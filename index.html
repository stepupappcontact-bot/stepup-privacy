StepUp - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

StepUp (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is a personal growth and productivity application designed to help users build habits, complete daily tasks, track mood and journaling, and visualize their self-improvement progress. Your privacy is extremely important to us.

1. Information We Collect

StepUp does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data to external servers. All data remains ONLY on your device.

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Tasks you create (title, category, repeat)
  • Habits and habit completion history
  • Journal entries including:
    • Text you write
    • Your mood selection
    • Optional photo you add (stored on-device only)
  • XP, streak, level, badges information
  • App settings (sound, reminders, theme)

No user accounts, emails, phone numbers, or cloud backups are used.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data is used strictly for the app’s functionality:

  • To manage tasks, habits, and journaling
  • To generate insights and statistics
  • To calculate XP, streaks, levels, and badges
  • To store settings and preferences

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally using:

  • SharedPreferences – small data (tasks, XP, settings)
  • Local device storage – journal photos

We do not upload data to any cloud server. We do not store or process any personal data externally.

4. Third-Party Services

StepUp does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cloud services.

No ads, no tracking, no user profiling.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with anyone. We do not sell data. We do not transfer data. Your information never leaves your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

StepUp does not contain harmful content, but it is not specifically designed for children. Users under 13 should use StepUp with parental guidance.

7. Security

All data is stored securely on your device. Because nothing is transmitted online, exposure risk is extremely low.

8. Your Choices

  • You may delete all tasks, journals, and habits inside the app.
  • You may uninstall the app to permanently remove all data from your device.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the application evolves. Changes will be reflected on this page.
